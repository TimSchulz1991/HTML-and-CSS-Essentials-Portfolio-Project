Features

Swipe for friends

With our Swipe feature you can easily browse through other user profiles nearby. Finding other parents and expecting parents to text or hang out whith has never been this fun and easy.

Find the right match with our filter

Our filter allows you to only see other users, which exactly match your search criteria. You can filter based on other users' age, gender, their childrens' age, and you can specify the maximum distance they can be away from you.

Ask your questions in our forum

Our forum provides an easy-to-use platform to ask all your questions regarding parenthood and pregnancy to our kind-hearted community. You can be certain that there is always someone else, who has been going through the same situations as you have, and who can help you out.
